The Master of Go

The Master was like a starved urchin in his appetite for games. listen Shut up in his room with his room games, he was doing his heart ailment no good.

Most professional Go players like other games as well, but the Master’s addiction was rather special. There was no end to his patience and endurance.

It was less as if he were playing to dispel gloom or beguile tedium than as if he were giving himself up to the fangs of the gaming devils .

The Master seemed to be lost in vast distances.
